YOGA THEORY
Considered an art, Yoga is becoming more and more popular and becoming a current trend. So what exactly is Yoga? What types of yoga are there? What are the benefits and effects of yoga?... In the article below, MindfulIntentions will provide from A to Z general information as well as answer questions related to the topic of what yoga is, let's find out!
WHAT IS YOGA?
Yoga is a very old body training discipline, originating from India. Initially, when it was first introduced into our country, many people were curious and thought that bending movements did not have much effect in keeping a healthy and beautiful figure. Many people believe that yoga is the practice of coordinating the body, mind and spirit through different body positions. Over many years, yoga has proven its remarkable effects on human health.

Yoga has many movements divided into levels from basic to advanced, for different learning subjects. Practicing yoga needs to be followed for a long time to get the maximum effect. Practicing flexible movements helps increase physical strength and refresh the mind.

Nowadays, there are more fitness centers forming and most of them open yoga classes to guide people in exercising. When practicing, you need to coordinate your body with difficult postures, breathing techniques, and meditation. Soul, body, mind become one. After a while, this practice helps calm the mind, reduce pressure, fatigue, and improve health.
WHERE DOES YOGA COME FROM?
Yoga comes from India, a country with a long cultural tradition that existed in the world more than 5,000 years ago. In the past, yoga methods and techniques were taught orally from masters and their disciples to the outside world.

Yoga achieves the goal of improving people, helping the spirit feel comfortable and relieved, eliminating worries. Helps the body become firm, supple, reduces signs of aging, purifies the body, and effectively detoxifies. Yoga therapy helps balance the nervous system, affecting the organs to function stably.

WHAT ARE THE PRINCIPLES WHEN PRACTICING YOGA?
- Perseverance and patience: Practicing yoga does not have to be done early in the afternoon to bring the desired results. If you pursue it for many years, you will not only have a beautiful and healthy body but also practice perseverance, patience and a more gentle personality.

- Relax properly: When exercising, you should relax your body and regulate your breathing to keep your mind as comfortable as possible.

- Use a yoga mat: Yoga practice involves many movements lying close to the ground, so use a mat to keep it clean. The mat prevents slipping, tripping, reduces friction that can injure the body, and maintains balance during movements.

- Warm up carefully and properly: The first 15 minutes before practicing you must warm up to start more difficult exercises.

- Scientific diet: Besides exercise, you need to have a reasonable diet to ensure your health and help improve your body shape.

-Breathe properly: Regulating breathing is important to help organs such as the lungs get oxygen properly.
HOW TO DISTINGUISH THE MAIN TYPES OF YOGA
Currently in the world, experts classify yoga as divided into 7 main types. Each type has its own characteristics, exercises and level of difficulty for different subjects.
-What is Hatha yoga?
This type of yoga combines two important techniques: breathing methods and postures to properly stretch the muscles. The teacher will guide you to practice difficult postures more easily such as handstands, standing on your hands, and controlling your breathing.
-What is Ashtanga yoga?
This type requires many movements and different positions at the same time. Focus on techniques to regulate breathing, focus memory, and regulate blood. Combine breathing and movement more smoothly.
-What is Vinyasa yoga?
This type of yoga harmoniously connects breathing and movement into gentle, flexible movements. Typical movements include sun salutations, balancing, back bending, stretching, sitting and resting positions.
-What is Shakti yoga?
This type of yoga is complex both physically and mentally, at a difficult level for those who have practiced for a long time. The purpose of this subject is to create abundant energy for the body, create strength, and help refresh the spirit. Focus on exercises, breathing techniques, mantras with sounds, and songs.

Shakti Yoga has many different movements, releasing stress and fatigue. Each teacher will develop different exercises based on basic knowledge.
-What is Twisting Yoga?
This type of yoga mainly focuses on twisting movements to help the body become more supple and flexible. Helps heart health, blood circulation, blood circulation and good breathing. The postures help the spine function better, strengthen the muscles in the front of the abdomen and back, and reduce body pain.
-What is beauty yoga?
Beauty yoga practice methods mainly help the body stay healthy, have a slim figure, and have smooth, rosy skin. Practicing many different and flexible movements creates excitement for practitioners to pursue for a long time.
-What is power yoga?
This type of yoga is quite difficult, combined with aerobic movements that take away a lot of the practitioner's strength. This subject requires a lot of exercise with a variety of postures, helping to improve health, body flexibility, dynamism, and reduce fatigue.
- What is hot yoga?
This exercise method is considered the secret to helping women stay young, have a supple body, and have a small figure. Practicing hot yoga maintains abundant energy for the whole day. You can combine exercises at high temperatures near 40 degrees Celsius and humidity of about 40% to still bring good results.
-Iyengar Yoga
The movements of this type are quite slow with many details, suitable for beginners. Combine belts, body parts, and pillows to create the most standard posture.
-Gentle Yoga
This type includes simple, slow postures that are more suitable for beginners. Effectively affects the muscle and bone systems so students enjoy participating. At the same time, it relieves pressure, helps the body relax and the spirit is excited.
-What is Kundalini Yoga?
This type tends to help people's spirit stabilize, relax, and relieve stress. The main exercises are meditation, breathing regulation, and chanting. This subject is suitable for the elderly, people who are tired or have mental problems.
-What is Yin Yoga?
This type integrates the principles of hatha yoga and qigong, focusing primarily on connective tissues. This subject has simple postures to release energy, performed with supporting props. Included are 35 different poses and variations.
-What are Bizarre yoga trends?
Bizarre yoga trends means strange Yoga trends. In addition to the types of Yoga introduced, this is the newest type of Yoga, a clever combination of types of Yoga, contagious and creative.
